Cybercrime Awareness

Cybercrime involves criminal activity conducted through computers, networks, online services, or digital devices.

Common Cybercrime Categories

Account Compromise
Phishing Attacks
Malware Infections
Ransomware

Cyber Safety Tips

Use strong unique passwords.

Enable multi-factor authentication.

Avoid suspicious links and attachments.

Keep software updated.
