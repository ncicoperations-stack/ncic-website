Honeytrap Awareness

Honeytrap schemes involve deceptive relationships or interactions used to manipulate victims into sharing money, personal information, or sensitive content.

Common Warning Signs

Rapid Emotional Attachment
Requests for Money
Requests for Sensitive Images
Blackmail or Extortion

Protect Yourself

Verify identities before sharing information.

Be cautious of sudden emotional relationships online.

Do not send money to people you have never met.

Preserve evidence if blackmail occurs.
