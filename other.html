Other Cyber Incident Reporting

Some cyber-related incidents may not fit within the standard reporting categories such as online scams, identity theft, financial fraud, cybercrime, honeytrap incidents, or child safety concerns.

Use this reporting option to submit information about other internet-related incidents that require review by the National Cyber Investigation Center.

Provide as much factual information as possible, including relevant dates, websites, usernames, or other identifying details that may assist in understanding the incident.

Examples

Suspicious Online Activity
Unknown Digital Threat
Unclassified Fraud
Other Concerns

Before Submitting

Provide as much detail as possible.

Include dates and platforms involved.

Preserve screenshots and records.

Describe why the incident is suspicious.
